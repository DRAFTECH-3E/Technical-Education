Process of Architectural Drafting

Design Development – The schematic design is refined with more detailed drawings that specify layouts, materials, sizes, etc. Mechanical, electrical, plumbing, and structural systems are coordinated.

Site Analysis and Programming – The architect analyzes the site where the building will be located, taking into account conditions like slope, vegetation, access to utilities, etc. They also determine the requirements and goals for the building based on the client’s needs.
